2024 U.S. Presidential Election

Winner: Donald Trump

Donald Trump has won the 2024 U.S. Presidential election, defeating Kamala Harris by securing the necessary electoral votes. With strong support in key battleground states, Trump successfully reclaimed the presidency.

Key Battleground States

Pennsylvania

Trump secured Pennsylvania with over 94% of the votes counted, adding 19 crucial electoral votes to his total.

Georgia

Flipping Georgia back to red, Trump garnered 51% of the votes, contributing significantly to his electoral count.

Wisconsin

With 80% of votes reported, Trump held a lead in Wisconsin, securing a close win with 51% of the vote.
